Being Human.

We are human, and more alike than different.

We seek love and belonging.

We need safety & security alongside freedom & exploration.

We're always being pushed and pulled.

Difficulty turns to ease and peace turns to friction.

We all make mistakes, face bad luck, and hurt in some way...

But healing is always an option.

The path out of suffering toward liberation,

peace and flourishing beckons all.

I can help you to find your way.

Pause. Reflect. Restore. Resume.

Services

1:1 Counselling

Online Sessions

Zoom sessions from the comfort of your own space. Counselling tailored to your needs, accessible wherever you are.

1:1 Counselling

Walk & Talk Sessions

Find reprieve in a natural environment. Movement, breath, and grounded conversation combine to create a unique healing experience. Johannesburg central parks such as Delta and Emmarentia are ideal venues. We will discuss details upon booking. Sessions are naturally weather dependent.

Couples Counselling

Online Sessions

Begin with private intake sessions for each partner (1 hour), followed by guided joint sessions on Zoom. A space to strengthen communication and deepen connection.

Corporate

The Reset Room

People bring their problems to work. Enhance the efficiency of your team by providing a space for team members to offload. Book me for a day and designate a private space in the workplace for individual team members to check-in and reset. Get better results by honouring the humanity of your team.
